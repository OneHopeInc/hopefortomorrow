What is the Hope for Tomorrow Book of Hope?

The Hope For Tomorrow Book of Hope is designed specifically for teens, addressing their most pressing questions about identity, relationships, and purpose. Rooted in God's Word, it offers biblical truth and practical guidance on the challenges young people face today.

An 8-Week Journey with Guided Conversations

The Hope For Tomorrow Book of Hope is structured as an 8-week program that can be used in schools, youth groups, or churches. Each week introduces a key theme teens are wrestling with today—paired with a leader's discussion guide to help you spark meaningful conversation and guide students back to Scripture.
